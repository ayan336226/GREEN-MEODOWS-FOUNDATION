About Us

Green Meadows Foundation is a non-profit dedicated to environmental sustainability and community development in Gilgit-Baltistan. We focus on tree plantation, environmental education, waste management, eco-tourism, and livelihood support for marginalized communities.

Who we are

We are a local, grassroots foundation combining community action with data-driven projects to restore green cover and improve public health.

Where we work

Our primary focus is Gilgit-Baltistan ‚Äî schools, villages, urban green belts, and public spaces are core intervention areas.

How we operate

Through partnerships, volunteers, and transparent impact reporting ‚Äî from concept to evaluation.

Vision

Transforming Gilgit-Baltistan into a green, clean, and sustainable haven for all ‚Äî where communities thrive in balance with nature.

We envision resilient landscapes, cleaner air, thriving local economies around sustainable practices, and an empowered citizenry committed to stewardship.

Mission

Our mission is to promote environmental sustainability, improve community health, and enable livelihood opportunities through education, action, and partnership.

Education & Awareness

Environmental literacy programs in schools and community workshops to build long-term stewardship.

Plantation & Restoration

Large scale native tree planting with aftercare and survival audits to ensure lasting impact.

Healthcare & Hygiene

Mobile health camps and hygiene campaigns to uplift public health alongside environmental action.

Objectives

Clear, time-bound targets that guide our work.

10 Million Trees by 2030

Mobilize communities, sponsors, and partners to reach a region-wide afforestation target.

Waste & Recycling

Implement sustainable waste management pilots and scale successful models.

Livelihoods & Eco-Tourism

Support small businesses and eco-tourism to provide sustainable income while conserving nature.

Programs & Initiatives
